About the Game

Number Roll Game is a game of luck! The game involves two players owning a "Number Roll Machine". When the player presses the "Roll" button, the Number Roll Machine generates a random number between 0 and 100. The number generated by the machine is counted as the score earned by the player in the round. After the completion of both player's turn in a round, the player having the higher score causes damage to the other player. The game computes the damage to the health of a player by computing the difference between the scores of both players in the round. The game comes to an end when a player runs out of health and declares the other player as the winner! Play the game with your friends and find out whether you can stay Alive
